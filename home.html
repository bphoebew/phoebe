PSEUDO

an online web game based on online predators and how to identify and approach one if one were ever put in the situation

Why I Did This?

I know many friends that are catfished. Since they were only teens, they didn't know the effects of talking to strangers online. They just wanted to escape from the world and have fun. Sometimes its not just fun though. I wanted to help others such as my friends and help them be caution when online. Therefore, my friend and I created a game where you will be placed in scenarios talking to possible catfishers

Contributions:

As I helped start the project, I was also involved in creating the game. I created everyone's profile, the tutorial, updated the blogs, and put in the scripts for the chat

Wishful Outcomes:

- That we can spread awareness showing online predators are real and ppl need to be aware

- Can add more characters and monetize the game

Just Coding Things

an all-girls organization dedicated to helping each other and others to know how to code

Why I Did This?

Seeing only a few amount of girls in my AP Computer Science Principles class, I was unsuprised. However, I quickly found out that many girls didn't want to take the class because they felt pressured that it was hard and too many stereotypes were put on girls in CS. I wanted to create a unique & supportive sisterhood for girls at WR who want to pursue Computer Science

Contributions:

As the founder and president, I try to make the club as inviting as possible. Every meeting i would make the slides and be the teacher for the whole club

Outcomes:

-In the 2018-2019 school year, we were able to help 8 girls to learn how to code, with no prior knowledge

-In the 2019-2020 school year, we opened our club up to guys who wanted to learn web development

S(HE)TEM

a online website that gives resources to young girls interested in the STEM fields

Why I Did This?

On the last two days at Kode With Klossy, everyone was paired up with a partner to make any type of website. My partner and I were both passionate about women empowerment. With this passion in mind, we decided to focus our website around giving girls more resources in STEM since we did not really see that through our schools.

Contributions:

- I wireframed the website to make it fun and interactive for anyone that is browsing through the website

- I researched projects and interviewed prominent women in STEM

Highlights:

- We've been able to reach out to great women like NASA engineers or Karlie Kloss

- We were able to branch out to local libraries and give talks

AZN Magazine

Why I Did This?

Ever since i was young, I was either guessed as a Japanese, Korean, or Chinese Asian girl. I'm none of those, I'm Thai.
